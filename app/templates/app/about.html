About La Cima Market

Proudly serving our community since 2007

Our Story

La Cima Market is a family-run business established in 2007. As one of the few stores in the rural Summit Street area - where the nearest Walmart is a 20 minute drive away - we have proudly served our community by offering accessible general goods, Latin American ingredients, and regional delicacies.

For over 18 years, we have been committed to providing essential services including:

  • Propane Refueling
  • Freshly Baked Goods and Tortilleria
  • Full-Service Butcher Shop
  • Deli and Prepared Foods
